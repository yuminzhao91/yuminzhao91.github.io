Research Projects

  • 2021-2022
    Seismic attenuation extracted from traffic signals recorded by a seismic sensor.
    National University of Singapore
    • Detected isolated traffic signals from seismic ambient noise data.
    • Extracted daily seismic attenuation statistically from hundreds of traffic signals.
    • Analyzed the potential factors which cause variations in seismic attenuation.
  • 2020-2021
    Urban Running Activity Detected Using a Seismic Sensor during COVID-19 Pandemic.
    National University of Singapore
    • Developed an algorithm and a python package for automatic footstep signal detection and runner count from urban seismic ambient noise.
  • 2020-2021
    Near-surface imaging with DAS ambient noise data.
    National University of Singapore
    • Developed a method to invert the near-surface shear-wave velocity model from both Rayleigh and Love waves with the dispersion spectrum inversion method.
  • 2019-2021
    On beamforming of ambient noise recorded by DAS and wave type identification.
    National University of Singapore
    • Simulated urban seismic ambient noise (geophone/DAS).
    • Tested the reliability of beamforming on DAS ambient noise under different source conditions.
    • Identified surface wave types in the DAS ambient noise data.
    • Developed an algorithm to invert the Rayleigh-to-Love waves (R/L) ratio from DAS ambient noise data.
  • 2019-2020
    Urban near-surface seismic monitoring using distributed acoustic sensing (DAS) (cocontributor).
    National University of Singapore
    • {"Processed the DAS quarry blast data"=>"remove the near-field noise (traffic, construction, etc.), extract the seismic interferometry, and estimate the surface waves phase velocities."}
  • 2018-2019
    Extracting subsurface information based on extremely short period of DAS recordings.
    National University of Singapore
    • Removed the near-field noise in the DAS ambient noise data.
    • Extracted the seismic interferometry and dispersion spectrum from 2-min DAS ambient noise data.
  • 2016-2017
    Multi-trace seismic data deconvolution.
    China University of Petroleum (Beijing)
    • Developed a multi-trace seismic data deconvolution method that is robust to noise and performs well in preserving the lateral continuity of the inversion result.
  • 2014-2016
    Inversion-based time-space domain random noise reduction.
    China University of Petroleum (Beijing)
    • Developed an inversion-based random noise reduction method in the time-space domain using the prediction error filter (PEF) calculated from the noisy data as the regularization term.
